The Cannabis Advisory Committee are subject-matter experts that advise the Department of Cannabis Control on regulations that:

  • Protect public health and safety
  • Reduce the illegal cannabis market

Committee members

Members represent key groups including:

  • Cannabis industry
  • Labor organizations
  • State and local agencies
  • Racially, ethnically and economically diverse populations
  • Public health experts
